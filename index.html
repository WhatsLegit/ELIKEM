Meet the Founder

Elikem Dotse
Diabetic Wound Care Specialist

Born and raised in Ghana, Elikem founded ELIKEM DOTS with a single mission — to bring professional, affordable diabetic wound care directly to the people who need it most. Having witnessed the devastating effects of untreated diabetic wounds in Ghanaian communities, he dedicated his career to changing that reality.

Today, ELIKEM DOTS serves patients across Ghana with certified wound dressing services, home visits, and a trusted pharmacy — all built on the foundation of compassion, expertise, and community.
